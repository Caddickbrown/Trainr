Why Morning Planning Matters for ADHD
Without structure, your day gets hijacked by whatever screams loudest. A 15-minute morning routine gives you control and reduces decision fatigue all day. This is your time to set intentions before the chaos begins.

Why Evening Wrap-Up Matters for ADHD
An evening routine prevents the "Sunday scaries" from happening every night. It creates closure, reduces anxiety about tomorrow, and helps your brain shift from work mode to rest mode. This is your time to acknowledge what you did and set yourself up for success.
1
Review What Got Done
‚è±Ô∏è 3-4 min

Checked off completed tasks
Noted any "wins" or progress made
Identified tasks that moved to tomorrow (without guilt)
2

‚è±Ô∏è 3-4 min

Put away materials not needed tomorrow
Set out anything needed for tomorrow morning
