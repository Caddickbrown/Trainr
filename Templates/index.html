üí° Tips for Using Templates Effectively

  • Start with the Quick Reference Guide: Print it and keep it visible. It's your daily decision-making aid.
  • Use Daily Planning every workday: Consistency is key for ADHD. Same routine = less decision fatigue.
  • Fill out Meeting Prep 1-2 days before important meetings: Not the morning of - give yourself time to prepare properly.
  • Keep a "Templates" folder in your work drive: Store completed PDFs for easy reference and patterns over time.
  • Don't aim for perfection: A 70% complete template that you use is better than a perfect one you abandon.
  • Adapt as needed: These are starting points. Modify them to fit your team's specific needs and culture.
  • Share with colleagues: If others find them useful, share! Good systems benefit everyone.
