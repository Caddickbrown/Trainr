üí° ADHD Success Strategies for Your Role

üß† External Memory Systems

Don't trust your brain to remember. Write everything down immediately. Use calendar blocks, task lists, and visual boards religiously.

‚è∞ Time Blocking

Block time for focused work BEFORE accepting meetings. Protect your peak energy hours (usually mornings) for complex analysis.

üéØ The Power of Three

Each day, identify only 3 must-do priorities. More than that leads to decision paralysis. Three is achievable and clarifying.

üì± Manage Interruptions

Use Do Not Disturb liberally. Check email on YOUR schedule (2-3x daily), not constantly. Batch similar tasks together.

üîÑ Build in Buffer Time

Always add 25% more time than you think tasks will take. Leave 15 min between meetings. Your brain needs transition time.

‚úÖ Templates Reduce Fatigue

Create templates for recurring tasks (email responses, analysis formats, meeting prep). Consistency reduces mental load.
